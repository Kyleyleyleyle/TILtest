Transforming the Instructional Landscape


“Welcome to TIL, a place where technology and innovation meet to make instructional spaces more empowering for the U of T community.”

TIL is a space of collaboration, community and learning space design that sits at the crossroads of multiple departments at U of T. TIL not only focuses on classroom renovations and technology enhancement, but it takes a more holistic approach to exploring best practices for learning space design, whether they be online or in-person.


Stakeholder engagement is central to TIL, and we are always looking for instructors and staff at U of T to become involved in our work. Those involved with TIL can receive support in piloting new and innovative technology and learning methods. Over the years, many teaching staff have found value in collaborating with the TIL community to develop their own accessible, creative, and innovative learning spaces.
