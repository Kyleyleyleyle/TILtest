About


Transforming the Instructional Landscape (TIL) is a hub of experimentation, learning, and collaboration that focuses on understanding how to create the best experience possible for students and instructors, no matter the learning environment they find themselves in. Since its establishment in 2017, TIL has re-designed an impressive 93 classrooms in 24 buildings across the St. George campus. These classroom re-designs have centered the voices of students, staff, and faculty every step of the way, through engaging more than 1,200 stakeholders over the past four years.


While classroom renovation and technology enhancement continue to be important TIL initiatives, the COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the integration of digital technology into teaching and pushed us to reimagine what learning spaces can look like. In response to the changes that academia has recently and rapidly undergone, TIL has shifted its vision to take on a more holistic approach to the design of learning environments.
