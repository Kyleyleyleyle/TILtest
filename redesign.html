Classroom Redesign


TIL’s Classroom Redesign initiative is a renovation project involving classroom spaces on the U of T campus. This project aims to meet the everchanging needs of instructors and students. As student and faculty demographics continue to evolve, new technology and emerging teaching practices are changing the scope of what a successful instructional space looks like. In response, TIL’s Classroom Redesign project constructs classrooms that support a diverse range of program offerings, while accounting for the diverse needs of classroom users. You can read more about the different aspects of our redesign methodology by clicking on the links below.
