Approach


TIL is composed of a diverse group of architects, researchers, technology specialists, and leaders. Our work finds common ground through the principles of design thinking. TIL’s emphasis on design thinking prioritizes empathetic and humanizing engagements with classroom users at U of T. We strive to center the individual and not the average, to foreground the importance of accessibility and emotional stakes, and to treat our stakeholders as partners in the design process. We have adopted this approach because we know that purpose-driven design can empower instructors and students to have more fulfilling experiences with U of T’s instructional spaces, and can build trust in the university community more broadly.


To learn more about design thinking and how it informs our work, visit our “Human Centered Design” page below. For a more practical look at how this approach informs the ways that we design instructional spaces, see our “Consultation Process Journey Map.”
